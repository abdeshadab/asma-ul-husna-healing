🖨️ Printable Cards

Beautiful cards of Allah's 99 Names for printing and daily reflection

📄 Print Instructions

  • Paper: Use A4 or Letter size paper for best results
  • Settings: Set margins to minimum in your browser's print settings
  • Layout: Choose "More settings" → "Options" → "Background graphics" for colors
  • Quality: Select highest quality for crisp text and borders
  • Cards: Each name prints on its own card for easy cutting
